"Highlights of QC club this year"

by "Team QC IIITDWD"

"Exciting news from the Indian Institute of Information Technology Dharwad! The Quantum Computing club has just wrapped up a unique 4-day workshop, many students participated and learned about the fascinating world of Quantum Computing. The workshop covered a wide range of topics, including an introduction to Python programming, mathematics for quantum computing, and quantum language.

On the last day of the workshop, the club held a Hackathon, where students put their newly acquired knowledge to the test. Congratulations to Abhigyan Niranjan for securing first place and Azmi Nabeel for coming in second. Well-done to all the participants!

We're thrilled to see so many students get a taste of Quantum Computing and learn about this exciting field. We wish all the participants the best in their future endeavors and look forward to seeing what they achieve in the world of Quantum Computing. Kudos to the Quantum Computing club at IIIT Dharwad for putting on such a fantastic event!"
